Project Overview

The team needed a quick way to view Sales Agent details without navigating through multiple records. This ensured that managers could oversee their teams more effectively, improving decision-making and productivity.

? The Challenge

Managers faced difficulty accessing Sales Agent information in Salesforce, digging for details such as the name, customer accounts handled, date of joining, and years of experience, which were scattered across multiple records and reports. This led to operational delays that negatively impacted other team workflows.

! The Solution

Visibility and efficiency challenges were resolved with creating a map using Lightning Web Components (LWC). A query was created to integrate Sales Agent records, ensuring real-time retrieval of key fields (Name, Customers Handled, Date of Joining, Experience). The map was placed on the homepage for easy access and readability with the tile format.
