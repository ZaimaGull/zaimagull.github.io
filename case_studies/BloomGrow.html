Final Design

The solution in action

The final design demonstrates a young girl having tea time with her rabbit while pouring tea into the cup. Tiny Tot's Daycare's mission is to help children grow into good adults that make a positive impact in the future. The business emphasizes good mannerisms and treating others with kindness, which is represented in the tea time with the rabbit. The colors green & brown are depicted as grounding effects resembling nature as nourishment, which will ultimately serve with the extension of Bloom & Grow when launched.

Key Learnings & Reflections

Discussions on Daycare's mission allowed for design process to stay focused on concepts that mattered.
Agreement on the final design was challenging due to back & forth communication between owner. Creating deadlines pushed with coming to a decision.
If I could do something differently with this project, I'd set stronger boundaries with the design prototypes. Instead of getting feedback from the owner initially, I'd create a survey and have clients complete it first then present it to the owner.
Skills utlized for this project involved Canva and Figma. The final design selected was from Canva.
